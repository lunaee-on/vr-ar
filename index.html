Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality

Virtual Reality (VR)

Definition: Virtual Reality (VR) is a fully immersive experience where users interact with a completely digital environment.

Recent News: Apple introduced the Vision Pro, a high-end mixed reality headset with advanced eye-tracking and spatial computing.

Uses: Gaming, training simulations, virtual tourism, therapy.

Augmented Reality (AR)

Definition: Augmented Reality (AR) enhances the real world by overlaying digital elements onto a live view.

Recent News: Meta is developing next-generation AR glasses for seamless digital integration.

Uses: Social media filters, medical training, retail, navigation.
